Data Deletion Instructions

If you have logged into our app using Facebook and wish to delete your data from our system, you can request data deletion by following these steps:

  1. Send an email to phuctuong.ngo@students.mq.edu.au
  2. Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include your full name and the email address linked to your Facebook account (if different)

Once we receive your request, we will delete your user data associated with Facebook login within 7 business days and confirm the deletion via email.

If you have any further questions or concerns, feel free to contact us.
